store private prompts, transcripts, or final promotion verdicts here.

- `session-memory-skill-system-comparison.v1.json` is the machine-readable
  reviewed comparison across no-skill, baseline direct, candidate direct,
  candidate retrieval, and candidate single-skill conditions.
- `session-memory-skill-system-comparison.v1.report.md` records the same claim
  boundary and the operator-readable conclusions.
